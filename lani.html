    Reading protocol

    1. Read title, abstract, figures, and conclusion first.
    2. Write the prerequisite map: equations, methods, assumptions.
    3. Reproduce one calculation or plot before believing the paper.
    4. Track what is established, speculative, or unresolved.
    5. Turn every confusion into a study card or lab challenge.
